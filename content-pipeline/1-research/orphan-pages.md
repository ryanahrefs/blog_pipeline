### Missing from competitors:
1. **Ahrefs Site Audit as solution** - No competitor mentions Ahrefs for finding orphan pages
2. **Prioritization framework** - Which orphan pages to fix first (traffic potential, backlinks)

4. **Orphan page audit workflow** - Step-by-step process from discovery to resolution
5. **E-commerce specific issues** - Product pages becoming orphans after category changes
6. **Large site considerations** - Enterprise-scale orphan page management

### Content angles to differentiate:
- Focus on Ahrefs Site Audit workflow (branded advantage)
- Include prevention alongside detection/fixing
- Add prioritization matrix for which orphan pages matter most
- Cover common causes during site migrations and redesigns
