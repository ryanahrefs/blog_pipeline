### Search Intent Analysis

**Primary Intent:** Informational

**Intent Signals:**
- Pinterest's official guide ranks #1 - users want authoritative how-to information
- Multiple "tips" listicles rank (SEJ, Semrush) - users want actionable advice
- Reddit/Quora discussions at positions 2, 6, 8 show users seeking real experiences and opinions
- PAA questions focus on "how to" and "what is"

**Secondary Intent:** Community validation
- Strong Reddit presence suggests users want to verify if Pinterest SEO is worth the effort

**Dominant Content Format:** Listicle / Tips Guide

**Format Signals:**
- Top ranking content uses numbered tips (12 tips, 7 tips)
- Guides are practical and actionable, not theoretical
- Official Pinterest guide is short (850 words) but ranks #1 due to authority
- Third-party guides are 2,000-2,800 words

**Recommended Approach:** Create a numbered tips listicle (8-12 tips) with practical, actionable Pinterest SEO tactics. Target 2,000-2,500 words. Include "what is Pinterest SEO" intro to capture definitional queries.
