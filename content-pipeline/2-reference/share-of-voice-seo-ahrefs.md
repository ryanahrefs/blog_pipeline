## Existing Ahrefs Content on Share of Voice

### 1. Main Blog Article: "What Is Share of Voice? How to Measure It Across Channels"
**URL:** https://ahrefs.com/blog/share-of-voice/

**Key Points:**
- Share of Voice (SoV) is a top-down metric that helps quantify brand awareness in market context
- Originally a paid advertising metric, now expanded to multiple channels
- Covers three main types: Search SOV, Social SOV, AI SOV

**Types of SOV Covered:**
1. **Search Share of Voice:** Percentage of all clicks for tracked keywords landing on your website
2. **Social Share of Voice:** Percentage of brand mentions vs. total market mentions
3. **AI Share of Voice:** Percentage of total impressions in AI/LLM responses vs. competition

**Business Impact:**
- Small brands must overspend to maintain market share
- Larger brands can maintain position with SOV lower than market share
- Correlation between SOV and market growth

### 2. Help Center Article: "What is Share of Voice (SoV) and how is it calculated?"
**URL:** https://help.ahrefs.com/en/articles/2072381-what-is-share-of-voice-sov-and-how-is-it-calculated

**Ahrefs Definition:**
"The share of voice (SOV) metric shows the percentage of traffic that your target website gets, compared to the traffic of other websites."

**Calculation Method:**
```
SOV = Total clicks your target receives from all Tracked keywords /
      Total clicks going to all results in the SERP for all Tracked keywords
```

**Example (Keyword: "Coffee beans" in South Africa, 5,000 total clicks):**
| Position | Domain | Clicks | SoV |
|----------|--------|--------|-----|
| 1 | coffeelovers.za | 1,500 | 30% |
| 2 | coffeemania.com | 930 | 18.6% |
| 3 | commonmancoffeeroasters.com | 860 | 17.2% |
| 4 | coffee.za | 450 | 9% |
| 5 | superbeans.za | 200 | 4% |
