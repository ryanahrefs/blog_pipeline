**Secondary Intent:** Tool Discovery (minor)
- TubeRanker tool ranks at position 5, indicating some searchers want a tool to use

**Dominant Content Format:** Comprehensive Guide

**Format Signals:**
- Top result (BU.edu) is a step-by-step educational guide
- 4/10 results are long-form guides (1,200 - 5,500 words)
- Guides from authority marketing sites (WordStream, HubSpot, Semrush) all rank
